# Project: document-drafter

## Goal
A voice application where the subject is a sales man who's driving around. He should get in his car, open up the voice application. And as he speaks about his sales meeting, he will automatically create a structured quote output that appears on the screen. After that is created and done, there should be a flow for him to get follow-up questions, and that should all be appearing both as text, but also spoken out with a text to speech API,so that he is able to improve his sales skills and be better at his next sales meeting.
Build a production-ready application that meets all specifications in the specs/ directory.
